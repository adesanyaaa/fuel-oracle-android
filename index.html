Introducing Universal Fuel Oracle.

Android app that helps you minimize your fuel expenses by answering when and where to fuel your car.

UFO saves you money by helping you take fueling decisions based on various factors:

  • Changing fuel rates
  • Different gas stations rates
  • Fuel tank status
  • Location
  • Route
  • etc

Key Features

  • Automatic detection of destination
  • Simple alert popups on top of you Navigation application

Requirements

  • An Android device with GPS support
  • A simple OBD II device.
  • Note that Currently only USA fueling stations are supported.
